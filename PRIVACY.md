# Privacy Policy

At TOREX, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data in full compliance with global privacy standards.

## 1. Information We Collect
We do not directly collect or store your financial information. All transaction and payment data are securely processed and handled by our Merchant of Record, Paddle.com. We only receive necessary order identifiers and email addresses to fulfill your product delivery and provide support.

## 2. How We Use Your Data
Your email address will only be used for:
- Delivering the digital products.
- Providing technical support or verified updates regarding the product.
- We will never sell, rent, or distribute your personal data to third parties.

## 3. Contact
If you have any questions regarding how your data is handled, please use our public repository tracker: **[GitHub Issues](https://github.com/torex-codex/torex-codex/issues)**. (Note: Do not post personal or payment information publicly. For financial data inquiries, contact Paddle directly).
